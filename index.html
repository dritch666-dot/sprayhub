About

About Spray Hub

Spray Hub is an independent agricultural chemical reference tool built for Australian farmers, agronomists, turf managers, and home gardeners. It provides fast, offline-capable access to product label rates, Safety Data Sheets, APVMA permits, and weed, pest and disease identification data.

What We Offer

Our database currently contains 242 registered products across 38 companies, 354 Safety Data Sheets, 680 APVMA permits, and identification records for 1,107 weeds, 474 pests and 487 diseases. The spray calculators cover tank mixing, cost comparison, container coverage and nozzle calibration.

Our Purpose

Spray Hub was created to give people in the field quick, reliable access to label information without needing mobile coverage. All data is sourced directly from registered APVMA product labels and official permit documents. The app works fully offline once loaded.

Install to Home Screen

Spray Hub is a Progressive Web App (PWA) — you can install it directly to your phone or computer home screen for instant, app-like access. It loads instantly, works offline, and doesn't use app store storage. All your data stays on-device.

Reporting Data Errors

Accuracy is critical. If you find a rate, withholding period, or pest/crop entry that doesn't match the registered label, please email us with the product name and the specific error. We will verify against the APVMA-registered label and correct it promptly.

Response Time

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 48 hours. Data error reports are prioritised.
